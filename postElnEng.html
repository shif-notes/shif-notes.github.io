Something about Electronics Engineering

Just a heartfelt piece from an Electronics Engineer

Posted on October 23, 2024

When I wanted to take admission in engineering I didn't know which field to choose and I ended up choosing Electronics Engineering.

As I went ahead with my studies I realized that Electronics Engineering is the study of both Hardware and Software.

If in one subject we were learning a programming language then in another we were learning circuits, it's like the best of both worlds of Engineering.

That's what I enjoyed the most incorporating both hardware and software in my college project. Assembling all the electronic components, connecting them with particular wires, installing code and checking if our small robot we made works accordingly or not. This I think is one of the wonders of Electronics Engineering where we can connect both hardware and software and make a tangible product.

Having studied in this branch had broadened my perspective of technology that there are so many things and so many other things that can be done.

From an Electronics Engineer
